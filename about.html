About

This convention was founded on the basis to inform people about video games and how the industry functions. We want to give people insight and get them interested in to this feild of work so we can extend the reach of gaming and gaming design. Here you will find the latest information of what goes in the gaming world and even get to try out some of the new games coming and even try out some new of the tech the companies are eager to show off.
