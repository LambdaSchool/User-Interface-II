ABOUT Eri Autumn Berry

EriAutumnBerry was founded in 2006! Created by Erica Droz, it is a brand name that identifies her, and somewhat of her personality. Name: Erica "Eri" Favorite season: Autumn hence "autumn" Favorite fruit: Strawberry hence "berry"

Identifying parts of her personality, she then created the name "EriAutumnBerry", which she has trademakred and made her own company. Her company is based off of the art work that she creates, however, it is not limited to that.

An expanding, yet, promising business has emerged, and she hopes to create fashion designs as well as the jewelry and Canvas art that she already creates!
